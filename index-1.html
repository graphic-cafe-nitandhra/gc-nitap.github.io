Agenda



Media being the 4th pillar of the 21st century, is the most intricate and diverse communication channel. Social media particularly has a foothold over the majority of impactful marketing that exists, prompting GC to stretch its reach in social media to intensify the prominence of NIT Andhra Pradesh and skip conventional methods.Digital partner GC with its wings of GRAPHIC DESIGN, PHOTOGRAPHY ,MUSIC ,WEB DEVELOPMENT AND DESIGN, SOCIAL MEDIA AND PUBLIC RELATIONS has planned out tasks for a period of 1 year to increase the number of people spotting NIT Andhra Pradesh.

GC has been meticulously managing Twitter, Facebook and Instagram pages and plans to vocalize fests and create a YouTube channel for telecast. To intensify its reach, creation of a TikTok ID open to all students is being planned. Formulation of a student gallery of artworks and photographs and an E book library portal comprising of lecture and student notes is also one of the key agendas. GC plans to create a web view android application for rapid launch of campus notifications. With the aforesaid plan of action, GC intends to put NIT Andhra Pradesh in the limelight.
